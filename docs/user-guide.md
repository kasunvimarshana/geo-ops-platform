## Getting Started

To get started with the GeoOps Platform, follow these steps:

1. **Download the Application**: Install the mobile application from the respective app store.
2. **Create an Account**: Register as a new user by providing the required information.
3. **Log In**: Use your credentials to log in to the application.

## User Roles

The application supports multiple user roles, each with specific permissions:

- **Admin**: Full access to manage the system and users.
- **Owner**: Manage business operations and customer interactions.
- **Driver**: Execute jobs and track GPS locations.
- **Broker**: Coordinate between farmers and service providers.
- **Accountant**: Handle billing, payments, and financial reports.

## Key Features

- **GPS Land Measurement**: Measure land accurately using GPS technology.
- **Job Management**: Create, assign, and track jobs through their lifecycle.
- **Billing & Invoicing**: Generate invoices automatically based on services rendered.
- **Payments & Ledger**: Manage payments and track customer balances.
- **Reports & Analytics**: Access various reports for insights into operations.

## Using the Application

### Measuring Land

1. Navigate to the **Lands** section.
2. Select **Measure Land**.
3. Walk around the perimeter of the land to capture GPS coordinates.
4. Review the measurement and save the data.

### Managing Jobs

1. Go to the **Jobs** section.
2. Create a new job by filling in the required details.
3. Assign the job to a driver and track its progress in real-time.

### Invoicing

1. After job completion, navigate to the **Invoices** section.
2. Generate an invoice based on the job details.
3. Share the invoice via email or WhatsApp.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I reset my password?**  
A: Go to the login screen and click on "Forgot Password." Follow the instructions to reset your password.

**Q: Can I use the app offline?**  
A: Yes, the application supports offline functionality. Data will sync automatically when you regain internet access.
